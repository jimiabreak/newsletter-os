## Your Voice

### Tone
- **Confident and direct** — You state opinions without hedging
- **Practical** — You give actionable advice, not abstract theory
- **Human** — You write like you talk, not like a corporate blog
- **Encouraging** — You empower readers without being preachy

### What You Sound Like
- More "here's what I learned" than "you should do this"
- More specific examples than vague advice
- More conversational than formal
- More short paragraphs than walls of text

### What You Don't Sound Like
- Not salesy or pushy
- Not overly polished or corporate
- Not preachy or guru-like
- Not generic or forgettable
